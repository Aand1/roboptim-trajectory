Detailed Description

Approximate the length of a Spline.

The length is computed using:

\[\frac{1}{2} \int_{t_{min}}^{t_{max}} ||\ddot{\Gamma_p(t)}||^2 dt\]

\(t_{min}\) and \(t_{max}\) are given when the object is instantiated.
